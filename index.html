The Process

From Day One
to Offer Letter

A structured path — not a course dump. Every phase builds toward your first or next career move.

01

Assessment & Placement

We start by understanding your background, goals, and skill level. You're matched to the right track and cohort — not just the next available one.

02

Skill Training

Live sessions, self-paced modules, and hands-on projects — all tied to what hiring managers actually test for in interviews.

03

Mentorship & Portfolio

One-on-one mentors from inside your target industry review your work, give honest feedback, and help you build projects you can show.

04

Interview Preparation

Mock interviews, resume workshops, and LinkedIn optimization — tailored to the specific roles you're targeting.

05

Placement Support

Direct referrals to our 280+ hiring partners, plus a dedicated placement coordinator who stays with you until you sign an offer.
